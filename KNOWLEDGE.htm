DISCOUNT & VAT

1) The total price of computer including 13% VAT is Rs 45200. Find the price excluding VAT.

Soll: Here, Rate of VAT =13%

Price with VAT = Rs 45200

Let,the price without VAT= Rs x

Then,x + 13% of x = price with VAT

or, 113%of x = 45200

or, 113/100*x = 45200

or, x= 40000

Thus, the price excluding VAT is Rs 40000.
